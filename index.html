How it works

Three things no one else does. All in one place.

01
🧠

Your AI companion

Monitors your HealthKit data, wearables, and labs continuously. Spots patterns before you feel them. Answers questions, orders labs, books appointments, and refills prescriptions — without you having to ask.

02
🩺

Your dedicated doctor

A real, licensed GP who sees your full health picture in real-time. Same doctor, every time. They already know your history, your patterns, your concerns — before every conversation begins.

03
⚡

Health running itself

Personal doesn't wait for you to open the app. It watches, learns, and acts — alerting your doctor, scheduling check-ins, and taking care of the logistics so your health is managed continuously.
